No account, no upload, no tracking of your footage. This page loads whisper (transformers.js) and ffmpeg (ffmpeg.wasm) as WebAssembly and runs the entire edit — transcription, cut planning, caption burn-in, rendering — locally in this tab. Closing the tab discards everything; nothing is sent anywhere. Source: github.com/Kawwshall/jumpcut
